Code of conduct

All attendees, speakers and volunteers of our conference are required to accept the following code of conduct. The organizers will enforce this code throughout the event. We look forward to the collaboration of all participants to help ensure a safe environment for everyone.

Our conference is dedicated to offering an abuse-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, size, race or religion. We do not tolerate abuse of conference participants in any form.

Abuses include: offensive comments regarding gender, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, size, race, religion, sexual images in public spaces, deliberate intimidation, harassment, persecution, photography or recording of abuse, continued interruptions of talks or other events, inappropriate physical contact and unwanted sexual attention.

Participants urged to stop any abusive behavior are expected to obey immediately.

Conference participants who violate these rules may be sanctioned or expelled at the discretion of the organizers.

If you are experiencing abuse, notice abuse of another person, or have any other concerns, please contact a member of the conference staff immediately.

The conference staff will be happy to help participants who are being abused to feel safe during the conference.
