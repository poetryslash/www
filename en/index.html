Hey, Poetry Slash is a recital of computational literature whose third edition will take place next Saturday, April 25 at La Casa Encendida, within the program of activities of the independent publishing fair Libros Mutantes.

Participants will read texts produced through code with the intention of exploring unconventional forms of literary creation and doing wonderful and crazy things with computers.

If you are reading this, please participate by sending your proposal before April 1st through this form.
